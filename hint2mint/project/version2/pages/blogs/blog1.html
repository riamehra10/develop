Are you guys confused about which sector to invest in ?

By Keshina Goyal


Due to the ongoing pandemic in the entire world the major four industries booming are:

  • Pharmaceutical industries
    With the virus prevailing there is an enormous demand for sanitisers, cleaners etc which makes this industry booming currently in this situation. Also, people are full of hope with the pharmaceutical industry to come up with the vaccine to fight against the CORONA virus.

  • Telecommunication industries
    Internet had always had a robust demand. But due to the widespread virus around internet use is reaching new heights. Today, even teachers from every school are teaching online and work from home trend have increased internet demand due to which the telecom industry is booming.

  • Chemicals industries
    With the rise in demand for sanitisers there comes the rise in demand for chemicals used as inputs for the manufacturing of the same and hence making this industry flourish even during this epidemic.
